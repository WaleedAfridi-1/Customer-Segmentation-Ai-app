Cluster Explanations

Click on any cluster card to learn more about its characteristics:

Cluster 0: Cost-Conscious

Customers who are careful with their spending and prioritize value.

  • Low to medium income levels
  • Budget-conscious shopping behavior
  • Respond well to discounts and promotions
  • Often research before making purchases
  • Value practicality over brand names
Cluster 1: Balanced

Customers who maintain a balanced approach to spending.

  • Medium income levels
  • Moderate and consistent spending patterns
  • Value both quality and price
  • Often loyal to brands they trust
  • Make planned purchases rather than impulse buys
Cluster 2: Premium

High-income customers who spend freely on premium products.

  • High income levels
  • Prefermium and luxury brands
  • Value quality, exclusivity and status
  • Less price-sensitive
  • Often early adopters of new products
Cluster 3: Savers

High-income customers who are careful with their spending.

  • High income but conservative spending
  • Focus on saving and investing
  • Value long-term benefits over immediate gratification
  • Research extensively before major purchases
  • Prefer value over luxury
Cluster 4: Risky

Customers with spending patterns that may be financially unsustainable.

  • Lower income but high spending habits
  • Prone to impulse purchases
  • Often influenced by trends and social pressure
  • May have credit management challenges
  • Respond well to "limited time" offers
